AI Subtitle Toolkit release packages include FFmpeg executables:

- ffmpeg.exe
- ffprobe.exe

The bundled Windows build is the FFmpeg 8.1.1 essentials build from GyanD/codexffmpeg.

FFmpeg is free software licensed under the GNU General Public License version 3 or later.
Run `ffmpeg.exe -L` or `ffprobe.exe -L` to view the bundled executable license text.
